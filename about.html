About CampusHub

CampusHub is a student-to-student marketplace empowering college students to buy, sell, and share services directly within their academic ecosystem.

Whether you're a coder building websites, a designer creating posters, a video editor, or someone who helps with assignments, CampusHub connects you with peers who need your skills.

We're reimagining college freelancing — one task at a time — and transforming how students collaborate, earn, and grow. All in one place. All by students. For students.
